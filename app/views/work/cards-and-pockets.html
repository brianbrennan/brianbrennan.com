Cards & Pockets

May 2014 — January 2016

Cards & Pockets is an online retailer that specializes in customizable, DIY wedding invitation. They receive over a million monthly visitors to their site, and as such, wanted to update the look of their site to something much more modern, maintainable, and capable of higher conversion rates.

View Site

To go hand in hand with their familiar business and arts-and-crafts style product, they wanted a soft pastel palette that would be reflective of the wide variety of colored paper products that the sold. On top of their entire redesign of their site, an entire backend service had to be built from the ground up as well. While the frontend is a melding of HTML, CSS, and JavaScript, the backend is a totally custom version of NOPCommerce, running on .NET.

The Old Version

The New Version

Flatter, with only the most important information being shown A simple way to show the complex inventory of 81 colors for a product And added Customer Gallery, to showcase the invitations made by users!
