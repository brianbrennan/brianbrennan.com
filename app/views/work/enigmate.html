Enigmate

Fall 2015

Enigmate is an encryption tool for every day use. While not robust enough for encrypting full files, enigmate is perfect for securing every day information. Running off of Enigma.js, enigmate uses ASCII based keys to encrypt and decrypt values, and is conceptually based off of the WWII era Enigma Machine.
